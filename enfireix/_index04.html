FAQ

Is the therapy safe?

Yes — non-invasive and performed by trained clinicians. Assessment needed for implants/pacemakers.

How many sessions are required?

Typically 4–8 sessions depending on condition; personalised during assessment.

Is it painful?

Most patients report minimal discomfort; device is designed for therapeutic use.

Who should avoid this therapy?

Certain implants or pacemakers — consult physician. Full assessment prior to treatment.
