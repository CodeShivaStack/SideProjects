Common conditions & why prompt treatment matters

Untreated spine and degenerative orthopaedic issues may progress, causing chronic pain, reduced mobility, nerve damage, or need for invasive surgery. Early regenerative therapy can slow or reverse progression.

  • Chronic back pain
    Reduced quality of life, sleep disruption
  • Failed back surgery syndrome
    Persistent pain after surgery; alternatives needed
  • Disc degeneration
    Loss of function, radicular pain
  • Non-union fractures & chronic inflammation
    Delayed healing, chronic pain

How untreated conditions can progress

  1. Initial injury/degeneration - minor pain & stiffness
  2. Chronic pain - functional decline, sleep disturbance
  3. Nerve involvement - radiating pain or neurological symptoms
  4. Mobility loss / surgery - invasive interventions may be required
Pulse IX Mega aims to intervene earlier with non-invasive regenerative therapy to reduce progression and help avoid surgery where possible.
